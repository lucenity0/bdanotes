Summary
This document outlines the academic syllabus and laboratory plan for a sixth-
semester course in Big Data Analytics at BMS College of Engineering. The
curriculum is structured into five units that guide students from the fundamentals of
digital data types through the practical use of NoSQL databases like MongoDB
and Cassandra, eventually mastering distributed processing with Hadoop and
Apache Spark. Students are expected to achieve specific learning outcomes by
engaging in hands-on programming tasks, such as managing large-scale datasets
and implementing MapReduce algorithms. Evaluation is conducted through a
balanced assessment framework consisting of internal tests, a laboratory
examination, and a final comprehensive theory paper that tests various cognitive
levels of understanding.

Keywords: Big Data Analytics, NoSQL Databases, Hadoop HDFS MapReduce,
Apache Spark RDD, Scala Programming Language
